## Why Accessibility Matters

Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) ensure that digital content is accessible to people with disabilities. For designers, this primarily involves **Color Contrast** and **Visual Clarity**.

### Understanding Contrast Ratios

Text must have sufficient contrast against its background.
- **WCAG AA**: 4.5:1 for normal text.
- **WCAG AAA**: 7:1 for normal text.

## Best Practices

- Don't rely on color alone to convey meaning (use icons/labels).
- Test your palette in grayscale.
- Use sufficient white space.
