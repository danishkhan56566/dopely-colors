## The Three Layers of Color

A robust design system has three layers of color tokens:

1.  **Primitive Tokens**: The raw values (e.g., `blue-500: #3B82F6`).
2.  **Semantic Tokens**: The meaningful names (e.g., `color-action-primary`).
3.  **Component Tokens**: Specific to elements (e.g., `button-bg-hover`).

## Why Layering Matters

Layering allows you to rebrand an entire app by changing just the semantic mapping, without touching every button component.
