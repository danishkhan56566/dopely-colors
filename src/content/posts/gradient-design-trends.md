## 1. Mesh Gradients

Mesh gradients create a fluid, organic feel. They are perfect for SaaS headers and feature cards.

## 2. Grainy Gradients

Adding noise/grain to a gradient adds texture and depth, removing the "digital" feel and making it tactile.

## 3. Holographic Effects

Iridescent colors (Cyans, Magentas, Silvers) are trending for "Web3" and "AI" products, giving a futuristic vibe.
