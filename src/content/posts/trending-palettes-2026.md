Color trends are cyclical, yet always evolving. As we look towards 2026, we're seeing a fascinating shift away from the muted minimalism of the early 2020s towards more expressive, digital-first aesthetics. Here are 5 palettes that capture the zeitgeist of tomorrow.

## 1. Digital Lavender & Cyber Lime
> **Keywords:** Wellness, Digital Serenity, Gen Z

This palette combines the calmness of lavender with the electric punch of high-vis lime green. It bridges the gap between digital wellness and metaverse energy.
*   **Primary:** `#E6E6FA` (Lavender Mist)
*   **Secondary:** `#D0F0C0` (Tea Green)
*   **Accent:** `#CCFF00` (Cyber Lime)
*   **Neutral:** `#1A1A1A` (Almost Black)

## 2. Galactic Cobalt
> **Keywords:** Exploration, Mystery, Luxury

Space tourism and AI exploration are influencing design. Deep, intense blues evoke the vastness of the universe, paired with metallic silvers and purples.
*   **Primary:** `#0047AB` (Cobalt Blue)
*   **Secondary:** `#191970` (Midnight Blue)
*   **Accent:** `#FF00FF` (Magenta)
*   **Neutral:** `#C0C0C0` (Silver)

## 3. Earthy Brutalism
> **Keywords:** Sustainability, Raw, Authentic

Sustainability goes hardcore. Think unbleached cotton, raw concrete, and deep forest greens. This is eco-friendly design with an edge.
*   **Primary:** `#F5F5DC` (Beige)
*   **Secondary:** `#2E4600` (Deep Moss)
*   **Accent:** `#D2691E` (Clay Orange)
*   **Neutral:** `#4A4A4A` (Concrete Grey)

## 4. Viva Magenta (Evolution)
> **Keywords:** Empowered, Fearless, Optimistic

Building on Pantone's Color of the Year 2023, this trend evolves into deeper, richer berry tones mixed with soft pinks. It’s warm, inviting, and bold.
*   **Primary:** `#BB2649` (Powerful Red)
*   **Secondary:** `#FFC0CB` (Soft Pink)
*   **Accent:** `#FFD700` (Gold)
*   **Neutral:** `#FFFFFF` (White)

## 5. Retro Futurism (1980s Redux)
> **Keywords:** Nostalgia, Vaporwave, Fun

Neon is back, but cleaner. Think *Blade Runner* meets *Apple Design*. High-contrast neons on dark backgrounds, but used sparingly for UI elements.
*   **Primary:** `#121212` (Dark UI Background)
*   **Secondary:** `#00FFFF` (Cyan)
*   **Accent:** `#FF007F` (Neon Rose)
*   **Success:** `#00FF00` (Terminal Green)
